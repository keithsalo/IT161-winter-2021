Audience Issues and Concerns Needs
Perspective Clients - These are homeowners who are either looking for a designer to work with them on a new construction project or on a home renovation
  • Examples of work
  • Understanding of scope
  • Understanding of process
  • Qualifications and credentials
  • Testimonials
  • Contact information
  • Contact page
  • FAQ page to explain scope and process
  • Easy way to navigate portfolio of past projects
  • About page provide qualifications and testimonials/reviews
Perspective Partners – These are builders or artisans looking to collaborate on a project who want to see if the design process and aesthetic are compatible with their own
  • Examples of work
  • Examples of other partnerships
  • Understanding of scope
  • Understanding of process
  • Qualifications and credentials
  • Testimonials
  • Contact information
  • Contact page
  • FAQ page to explain scope and process
  • Easy way to navigate portfolio of past projects
  • Easy way to navigate portfolio of past collaborations
  • About page provide qualifications and testimonials/reviews
Returning Clients – These are past clients that may want to return to the site to reference images of their project, show the portfolio as a recommendation or submit a comment
  • Examples of work
  • Ability to submit and or share a review
  • Contact information
  • Contact page
  • Review form page
  • Easy way to navigate past projects
